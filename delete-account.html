Users can permanently delete their account directly within the Achievement App.

Steps to delete your account:

  1. Open the Achievement App.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap on "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm your request.

What happens after deletion?

  • Your authentication account will be permanently deleted.
  • All associated user data, including achievements and profile information, will be permanently removed from our systems.
  • This action cannot be undone.
